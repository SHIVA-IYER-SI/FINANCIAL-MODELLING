EQUITY VALUATION PROJECTS

NSE IPO VALUATION

This project involved a rigorous, institutional-grade valuation of the unlisted NSE, requiring a custom financial modeling approach. I built a multi-stage Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) model and performed a detailed Comparable Company Analysis (CCA) to establish a defensible intrinsic and relative valuation range. This work successfully translated complex financial statement forecasts and operational data into clear, actionable investment recommendations.

The final deliverable was a high-impact Equity Research Report detailing the investment thesis, key risks, and growth drivers. I leveraged Microsoft Excel for dynamic scenario analysis and utilized professional visualization techniques to assess the sensitivity of the final valuation. The objective of this project was to apply the highest standard of analytical rigor to produce concise, decision-ready reports.
